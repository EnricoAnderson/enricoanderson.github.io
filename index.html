Hey, I'm Enrico

Game Designer

Passionate game designer and creative writer from Indonesia with over six years of industry experience.

I specialize in system design and crafting meaningful player experiences. My work includes game balance, story writing, and content creation, covering puzzle design, level design, and narrative design.

I've worked both as a team member and as a lead for small groups of programmers and artists, guiding projects from concept to completion.

Published Works

As I Began to Dream (2025)

As I Began to Dream is my first major project, developed over three years early in my career as a game designer.

It’s a narrative-driven puzzle game that blends logic-based puzzle with visual storytelling. You play as Lily, a young girl trapped in her dream world after losing both of her parents. As you explore, you meet new companions and gradually uncover the mystery behind her situation.

My contributions to this project include creative direction, narrative design, system design, and level and puzzle design.

Seaside Tale (2026)

Seaside Tale is my most recent project, developed over the past eight months.

It’s a life simulation game focused on fishing and starting a new life in a remote seaside town. You can catch a wide variety of fish, upgrade your tools and income opportunities, build relationships with locals, and experience their individual stories.

My contributions to this project include creative direction, narrative design, system design, and game balance.
